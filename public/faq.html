Frequently asked questions


  • How long does a typical transaction take to go through?
  • If you're using a Coinbase account, it should go through in a few seconds. If you're using a non-coinbase wallet, it can take up to an hour.

  • What happens if the transaction fails?
  • We notify you of the failure (through a penny charge on Venmo), and return your money to you via Venmo.

  • Why do I keep getting $0.01 charges?
  • We use penny charges to notify you of success or failure. Feel free to reject or ignore them.
